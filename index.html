About Ciara...

A Mezzo Soprano Singer from Scotland

Ciara Harvie is a stunning and impressively talented mezzo-soprano. The 21-year-old Scot is one of the brightest stars emerging on the music scene with her unique and exquisite classical tone. Adaptable and appropriate in any environment, her profile is quickly growing through a range of television, concert and theatre appearances, as well as performing at corporate and bespoke private events around the world.

Ciara, from Longniddry, East Lothian has a unique background. She taught herself to sing watching Youtube videos of other classical singers such as: Jackie Evancho, Katherine Jenkins and Andrea Bocelli. After wowing her family, she signed up by the renowned Russian vocal coach Sergei Desmond and has progressed immeasurably since. Power is one of Ciara’s greatest assets. Her voice has the ability to hit notes many others can only dream of. The atmosphere and raw emotion her concerts create are breathtaking and leave audiences stunned.

Worldwide Audience

Ciara’s reputation is now global, with regular appearances in Europe and beyond as the world continues to be wowed by this young operatic singer possessing a voice filled with such passion, warmth, soul and maturity.

Past Performances

Her fan base is rapidly growing through various performances at venues such as The Edinburgh Fringe Festival, Children’s Charity Spifox Annual Lunch, while her biggest performance to date was on The Voice UK 2017. This lead to her being invited to represent the UK in a classical concert in Moscow at The Tchaikovsky State Conservatory, performing live with The Tyumen Orchestra.
